FAQs

How many volts are used in Taniti outlets?:

Power outlets are 120 volts (the same as in the United States).

What is the legal drinking age?:

The drinking age on Taniti is 18 but the drinking age isn't strictly enforced. Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m.

Will I be ok only knowing English?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.

What if there is a medical emergency?:

There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

Is Taniti safe?:

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti, but as tourism increases, there are more reports of petty crimes.

How is holiday travel on the island of Taniti?:

Taniti celebrates a large number of national holidays. Many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.

What is the currency used in Taniti?:

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
