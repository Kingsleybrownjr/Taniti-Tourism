Things To Do...

The Eats

There are over 100 places to eat on Taniti island, including 60 bars. No matter what your preference are, Taniti restaurants cater to guess from all walks of life. Enjoy Asian fusion, Italian, Mediterranean, vegan, fish, and many more. Taniti also houses it's own brewer, so don't miss out on our local, prize-winning beers.

Outdoors

With Taniti island expanisive and luxurious coastline, you'll be sure to find the perfect outdoor adventure. Hiking, fishing, and our Seven Mile beach are all popular attractions. Don't forget to check out the Haiku Waterfall located near Fantasy Cove.

Night Life

Taniti island boast a hot nightlife. It is one of the hottest places to be once the sun goes down. Catch a local show, a DJ, or even a rocking dance club. There are no "last calls" on this island. All our bars and clubs are open until sunrise. After all, who sleeps?
